# Flow Balance: Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for your trust in using **Flow Balance**. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to **ZMR**. This Privacy Policy details how information is managed, collected, and protected during the use of our application.

By using Flow Balance, you accept the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Nature and Data Collection

### 2.1. Offline Application and User Data
**Flow Balance is an *offline* application by nature.** This means that **we do not collect personal data** or financial information directly from users through the application. All financial data entered by the user is stored **exclusively locally on their mobile device**.

### 2.2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services
Although Flow Balance does not collect personal data, the application uses third-party services focused on stability and usage analysis. These services may automatically collect information, according to their own policies:

| Service | Type of Data Collected | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Google Analytics** | Aggregated usage statistics (e.g., time of use, devices used, navigation flow). | To analyze application usage to improve its functionality and user experience. |
| **Crashlytics** (Firebase) | Diagnostic data and device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, installation ID) when an error occurs. | To monitor stability and report application crashes for error correction. |

**Note:** The data collected by these services is anonymous, used in an aggregated manner, and is not linked to a specific personal identity of the user.

## 3. Use and Purpose of the Information

The information collected by third-party services is used exclusively for:

* **Service Improvement:** Analyzing application usage statistics to identify areas for improvement and optimize functionality.
* **Stability and Diagnosis:** Identifying and correcting bugs or failures by analyzing Crashlytics reports.

## 4. Protection and Security of Information

We ensure that data collected by third-party services is managed using the security standards provided by Google and its associated services (Firebase).

**ZMR is committed to not selling, renting, or disclosing personal information or application usage data to third parties, beyond what is established by the policies of the integrated analytics services (Google Analytics and Crashlytics).**

## 5. Changes to this Policy

**ZMR** reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Modifications will take effect immediately upon their publication on this page. We recommend users review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we manage information.
